# Guide

Welcome to the Retentio guide.

Retentio is built around spaced repetition: review what matters at the right time, so you remember more with less wasted effort.

In this guide, you will learn:

- what spaced repetition is and why it works
- how Retentio schedules reviews
- how to build a practical study workflow you can keep long-term

- [What is Retentio](/guide/background/what-is-retentio)
